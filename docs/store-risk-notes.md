# Store Risk Notes

This RC is not intended for Google Play or AdMob as-is.

## Main Risks
- Explicit sexual/kink text is bundled directly in locale files.
- Fire-tier cards include terms and themes that app stores and mainstream ad networks may reject or restrict.
- AdMob may limit or block monetization around sexually gratifying content.
- A Play Store build would need separate review-safe content and Android-specific ad integration.

## Recommended Store Path
Create a separate `Ignight Play Edition` later if store distribution becomes important.

That edition should:
- Tone down explicit language.
- Remove direct fetish/CNC/free-use/pee-kink cards.
- Use Google Play Billing for digital unlocks.
- Use the Google Mobile Ads SDK only if the content passes AdMob policy.
- Keep the hot edition on `ignight.me`.
