# Ignight Launch Notes

Ignight is the hot web/PWA edition: explicit, local-first, multilingual, and tuned for mobile play at `ignight.me`.

## Included
- 18+ age gate with local confirmation storage.
- Privacy, terms, adult-content notice, and contact modals.
- Vendor-neutral analytics event queue at `window.IgnightAnalytics.queue`.
- Optional native sponsor slots on the game selector and results screen.
- Sponsor configuration override through `window.IgnightSponsors`.
- Ritual progression, embers, unlocks, swipe tutorials, music, and card decks.
- Lightweight multiplayer and webcam relay endpoints for the Never Have I Ever prototype.

## Not Included
- Google Play / AdMob integration.
- Payment processor integration.
- User accounts, cloud saves, or payments.
- Play-safe content rewrite.

## Launch Posture
Ship the web/PWA first. Measure retention, session depth, deck completion, multiplayer stability, sponsor-click behavior, and whether any monetization damages the mood.
